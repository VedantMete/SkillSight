OBJECTIVE

of this project

One big aim of our project will be to completely avoid bias. Interview bias

occurs when the interviewer judges a candidate not only on their skills

and competencies but on unspoken or unconscious criteria hence making

the interview less objective. Our web application will judge candidates pu-

rely on the basis of their competence.
